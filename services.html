A message from the President to you.

Ghana Cahrity Foundation!.

Growing up as a kid I always wounder why we had people beggin and sleeping on the streets. I thought to myself, "It doesn't make any sense". Later in life I have come to the realization that not all fingers are equal, but still it hurts to see people having to eat once or not having to feed in a day. It hurts to see people sleep on the street. It hurts to see people not being able to cloth themselves or even afford basic social amenities. All of these are things that are painful experiences. few of my friends and I caem together to start this foundation. We might not be the riches set of people on earth but then we try to make someone happy every day. I arge you today, be your brothers keep and always make someone happy.The world is a better place with happier people!
